### Create Your Own Playlist

#### Step 1: Starting Songs

Start with our [Best 50 Tango Songs](/en/blog/best-tango-songs) list.

#### Step 2: Tanda Format

To create a playlist like professional milonga DJs:

```
4 Tango (same orchestra)
1 Cortina (30 sec different music)
4 Tango (different orchestra)
1 Cortina
4 Vals (mixed orchestra)
1 Cortina
3 Milonga (mixed orchestra)
1 Cortina
... repeat
```

#### Step 3: Energy Wave

Organize your playlist in a "wave" shape:

```
Medium energy ‚Üí High ‚Üí Romantic ‚Üí High ‚Üí Medium
```

This provides ideal flow for a 2-3 hour milonga.

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Electro Tango

Groups like Gotan Project, Bajofondo make great music but are not played at traditional milongas. These are for groups dancing "Tango Nuevo."

<Callout type="warning" title="Milonga Rule">
Only Golden Age (1935-1955) music is played at traditional milongas. Electro tango or modern tangos are not played.
</Callout>
