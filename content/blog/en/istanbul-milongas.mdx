Istanbul is Turkey's tango capital. Dozens of milongas, workshops and events take place every week on both the European and Asian sides. In this guide, we map out Istanbul's tango scene.

<GamzeQuote>
There's a milonga you can dance at every night in Istanbul. You just need to know where to go!
</GamzeQuote>

## European Side Milongas

### Beyoğlu - Taksim Area

The heart of Istanbul's tango scene. Most milongas are in this area.

**Featured Venues:**

#### 1. Tango İstanbul
- **Address:** Beyoğlu
- **Days:** Wednesday, Friday, Saturday
- **Time:** 21:00 - 01:00
- **Floor:** Parquet, medium size
- **Atmosphere:** Classic, traditional
- **Level:** All levels

#### 2. Milonga del Sur
- **Address:** Galata
- **Days:** Tuesday, Thursday
- **Time:** 21:30 - 00:30
- **Floor:** Wood, spacious
- **Atmosphere:** Intimate, warm
- **Level:** Intermediate-advanced

#### 3. La Cita
- **Address:** Near İstiklal
- **Days:** Friday, Saturday
- **Time:** 22:00 - 02:00
- **Floor:** Spacious
- **Atmosphere:** Energetic
- **Level:** All levels

### Beşiktaş - Nişantaşı

More elegant and modern venues.

#### 4. Tango Nişantaşı
- **Days:** Thursday
- **Time:** 20:30 - 23:30
- **Atmosphere:** Sophisticated
- **Level:** Intermediate-advanced

### Bridge to Kadıköy: Karaköy

Europe-Asia connection.

#### 5. Karaköy Tango
- **Days:** Wednesday
- **Time:** 21:00 - 00:00
- **Atmosphere:** Artistic
- **Level:** All levels

## Practicas

<TangoTerm term="Practica" definition="Informal dance environment for practice purposes. Ideal for learning and experimenting." />

### Why Practica?

- Try new figures
- Don't be afraid of making mistakes
- Ask questions
- Less pressure

### Weekly Practicas

| Day | Venue | Time | Feature |
|-----|-------|------|---------|
| Monday | Beyoğlu Practica | 20:00 | Class + practica |
| Tuesday | Kadıköy Practica | 19:30 | Technique focused |
| Sunday | Moda Practica | 15:00 | Afternoon |

<Callout type="tip" title="Beginners">
Go to a few practicas before your first milonga. Get to know the environment, meet people.
</Callout>

## Outdoor and Special Events

### Summer Milongas

Outdoor tango in Istanbul summers:

- **Bosphorus waterfront milongas** - June-September
- **Park milongas** - Weekends
- **Terrace milongas** - Various venues

### Festivals and Marathons

Throughout the year in Istanbul:

1. **Istanbul Tango Festival** - Summer
2. **Bosphorus Tango Marathon** - Autumn
3. **New Year Milonga** - December 31
4. **Valentine's Milonga** - February 14

### Special Nights

- **Live orchestra nights** - Monthly
- **DJ showcase** - Various
- **Themed milongas** - Vintage, 30s, etc.

## Practical Information

### What to Bring

- Tango shoes (or comfortable dance shoes)
- Change of shirt/top
- Water bottle
- Cash (most places)
- Positive attitude!

### Getting There

- **European side:** Metro to Taksim/Şişhane
- **Asian side:** Ferry + Metro or bus
- **Late night:** Taxi or rideshare

### Costs

| Type | Price Range |
|------|-------------|
| Regular milonga | 150-250 TL |
| Practica | 100-150 TL |
| Special events | 250-400 TL |
| Festivals | Varies |

## Conclusion

Istanbul offers a vibrant tango scene that spans two continents. Whether you're a beginner looking for your first practica or an experienced dancer seeking traditional milongas, there's something for everyone. Start exploring and become part of Istanbul's tango community!
