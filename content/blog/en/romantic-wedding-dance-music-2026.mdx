## What Should You Pay Attention to When Choosing Music?

1.  **Duration:** Ideal dance duration is **2.5 - 3 minutes**. If your song is 5 minutes, guests might get bored. We cut and arrange (edit) the song for you in our lessons.
2.  **Rhythm:** "A song we love very much" may not always be "a danceable song". Its rhythm should be holdable. You can consult me for this.
3.  **Lyrics:** If you are choosing a foreign song, **definitely check the lyrics.** The music might be great but it might be describing breakup pain, not suitable for a wedding! üòÖ
