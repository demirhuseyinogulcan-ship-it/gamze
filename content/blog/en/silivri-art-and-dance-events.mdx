Istanbul... The city we fall in love with but sometimes get tired of. Horn sounds, never-ending constructions, crowds of people...
Sometimes one just looks for **silence** and **aesthetics**.

Exactly at this point, **Silivri**, the western gate of Istanbul, offers us a wonderful shelter. But not only with its sea and meatballs; now with its developing **art and dance ecosystem** too.

As a Silivri resident and art educator, I watch this transformation in the culture-art life of our district with pride and as Gamze Tango, I take part right in the heart of this transformation.

## Silivri is Changing Shells

Silivri, which used to be a summer resort visited "from summer to summer", now has a population that lives, produces, and is hungry for art for 12 months.

*   Theaters in the culture center,
*   Painting workshops,
*   And of course increasing **dance studios...**

People no longer want to be stuck in malls to socialize, they want to do activities that will nourish their souls.

## Why Tango in Silivri?

Going to a tango course in the center of Istanbul can sometimes turn into torture. Rush hour traffic, parking problem... your energy spared for dance runs out on the road.

However, time flows **slower** and **with higher quality** in Silivri.

When you come to our studio, you don't spend half an hour looking for a parking spot. You park your car, inhale the sea air, and start the lesson "soft as cotton". Tango requires a calm mind and Silivri offers this calmness to you naturally.

How about coming to Silivri on Saturday, taking your walk/breakfast on the beach during the day, and joining our **Tango Workshop** in the afternoon? Both vacation and art, together.

## What Do We Do As Gamze Tango?

We don't just "give lessons and send away" in our studio in Silivri. We are trying to make this place a **cultural meeting point**.

*   **Movie Nights:** We watch tango-themed movies and chat about them.
*   **Music Recitals:** We explain the history of tango music, instruments (bandoneon).
*   **Practice Hours:** Our door is open to all dance lovers on certain days of the week; come, drink our tea, dance, watch.
